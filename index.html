turn android app

It's a very simple logic/thinking game:

  • The goal is to make every emoticon happy
  • You can turn the emoticons
  • You can only turn a certain number at a time

At the moment it's not translated to english. LICENSE: http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/3.0/deed.de Emoticons by: https://www.iconfinder.com/phdieuli
